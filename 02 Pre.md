# Pre-Production

## Is the Work Worth Recording?

### Works Not Worth Recording
* Bibles
* Children's Books
* Fanfic
* "Foreign" Language.
* "Hot" Books

### Works That *Might* be Worth Recording
* Magazines
* Periodicals
* Serialized Works

---

## How to Read and Record
### All Books Must Be
* Clear
* Consistent
* Coherent
